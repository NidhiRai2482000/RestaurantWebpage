Waiter Service

More commonly known as sit-down service, a waiter takes care of everything from taking orders to serving food and payment.

Buffet Service

In a buffet service, guests can choose their food from a wider selection made available on the buffet line.

Room Service

Guest make food and beverage order from the room telephone, interactive television or hotels mobile app. For small orders the room service tray is properly laid out is used for serving the guest.

Self service

Diners place, pay and pick up their orders at a counter similar to fast casual and fast food establishments.

Semi self-service

Similar to self-service, diners order and pay for their orders at the counter but their food is served to them when ready
